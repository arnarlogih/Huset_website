May 10th the youth magazine SEIN is having a preview of the new documentary serie ‘Watch me when you sleep’ about the alternative hiphop-duo FRAADS. A night of celebration, movie screening, Q&A, concert and DJ’s!


The documentary has been directed by Mads Salbæk and Tessa Hoder, produced in collaboration with SEIN and the Danish Film Institute. The show is about Frederik and David that together make up the band FRAADS.


‘Watch me when you sleep’ has online premiere 11th May which will be celebrated in a special manner the day before. Tickets are limited so buy your tickets ahead of time for the show to be sure to attend!
